Build 2013

By Steven Hylands
7th November 2013

Rumble Labs are proud to be sponsoring the 5th and final Build Conference in Belfast. We’ve attended every year and want to help it go out with a bang.

To do so, we’re inviting Build attendees to pop into the Rumble Laboratory on Wednesday, 13th November, for a bit of craic! Here’s what we have planned:

2:00pm to 5:00pm - Open Office

  • Table Tennis - Play a game of two on Rumbledon Centre Court with fellow attendees or take on one of the experienced Rumblers if you fancy a hiding.
  • Mario Kart - We’ll have our N64 loaded up with Mario Kart, relax and race in our lounge area.
  • Workspace - We have plenty of free seats and space, come along for the Wi-Fi and catch up on some work or hack with fellow attendees.

5:00pm - 6:30pm - Lightning Talks

We usually host Belfast Ruby once a month in the Lab, so this month we thought it would be great to broaden the scope and invite any Build Attendees to talk about whatever they like.

There will be a projector for any slides, lots of keen listeners and a strict 5 minute time limit per talk. If you’re interested in speaking drop us a mail.

Food and Drinks

There will be plenty of beers in the office and we’ll order in some food too. Afterwards we’ll be heading off to the Hudson for the last ever Standardistas’ Open Book Exam, and the Build Craft Beer Festival.

The Rumble Laboratory is located at 46 Hill St, in the heart of Cathedral Quarter, near The Mac, where the conference will be taking place. We look forward to seeing you!
